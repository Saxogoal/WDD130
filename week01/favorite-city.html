My favorite city is Paris, the capital of France. Known as the "City of Light," Paris is famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. The Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral are just a few of the iconic landmarks that make Paris a must-visit destination. The city's charming streets, world-class cuisine, and artistic heritage create an unforgettable experience for visitors.
